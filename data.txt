YES International College is located at Level UG, Block C, Megan Avenue 2, Jalan Yap Kwan Seng, Kuala Lumpur.

Their classes are held on Level LG, Level 8, Level 11, and Level 18 of the same building. Additionally, the COLLA Department and Culinary Program Facilities are located in Block B.
